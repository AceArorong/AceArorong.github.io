About Me

SKSU Computer Science Student

Greetings! My name is Ace Arorong, a native of Koronadal City, South Cotabato. I completed my elementary education at Marbel 5 Central Elementary School and subsequently attended Notre Dame of San Jose for both junior and senior high school. Driven by an innate curiosity about technology, I initially pursued Computer Science at AMA Computer Learning Center. However, I was somewhat dissatisfied with the quality of their academic services. Consequently, I transferred to Sultan Kudarat State University to continue my studies in this field. I am now immensely pleased with the quality of education I am receiving here.


I have actively engaged in numerous tech-related trainings and seminars alongside my former classmates. Notably, I have had the valuable experience of presenting a project proposal at the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) for a pitching contest, where my team achieved first place.
